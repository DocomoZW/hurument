ZINARA Board Management System Technical and Operational Specifications

Board Management System (BMS)

, as detailed in the procurement requirements for the Zimbabwe National Roads Administration (ZINARA), is a

cloud-based platform

designed to facilitate the end-to-end delivery, configuration, and ongoing support of board-related activities \[1, 2\]. The system is intended to streamline governance by automating meeting preparations, enhancing security, and ensuring regulatory compliance \[2, 3\].

Core Functional Capabilities

The system provides a suite of tools to manage the entire lifecycle of board meetings and corporate governance:

#### Meeting Management:

The Board Secretary can

create, schedule, and distribute electronic meeting packs

(agendas, reports, and minutes) in various formats such as Word, PDF, and PPT \[4\]. It includes

intuitive drag-and-drop tools

for building agendas and automatically sends calendar invitations to members \[4\].

#### Collaboration and Annotation:

Board members can

securely annotate documents

(highlighting and note-taking), with these annotations synchronized across devices while remaining private unless explicitly shared \[4, 5\].

#### Mobile and Offline Access:

The system must include a mobile application for

Android and iOS

that supports viewing and synchronizing pre-downloaded meeting packs in

offline or in-flight mode

#### Resolutions and E-Signatures:

A dedicated module captures formal resolutions and action items, assigning them to owners with mandated due dates \[5\]. To ensure legal validity, the system supports a

secure, legally compliant electronic signature process

for signing minutes and resolutions \[5\].

#### Governance and Transparency:

The BMS maintains an

immutable, date-stamped audit trail

for the entire lifecycle of a resolution \[5\]. It also features automated reminders for upcoming meetings and expiring action items, as well as an

emergency alert feature

for the Board Secretary to notify members of critical updates \[7\].

#### Voting and Reporting:

The platform enables formal votes or informal polls with immediate result aggregation \[7\]. It also generates

customizable reports

on governance metrics, such as attendance records and resolutions passed, to meet regulatory requirements \[3\].

Technical and Security Standards

Given the sensitive nature of board documents, the system must adhere to rigorous technical specifications:

#### Security and Encryption:

The provider must maintain

ISO 27000 certification and SOC 2 Type II compliance

\[8\]. Data must be encrypted using

TLS 1.2+ in transit and AES-256 at rest

#### Data Residency and Compliance:

All data must be hosted within a

data centre located in Southern Africa

and comply with the

Cyber and Data Protection Act of Zimbabwe

#### Performance and Reliability:

#### The system is required to have a minimum

99.99% uptime

\[8\]. Document loading times must be nearly instantaneous, with a requirement to load a

300-page meeting pack in less than 5 seconds

#### Scalability:

The architecture must support at least

100 concurrent users

10TB of archive data

#### Integration:

The BMS should integrate seamlessly with third-party platforms like

Microsoft Teams, Outlook, Zoom, and SharePoint
