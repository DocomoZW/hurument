01 — The Problem

Zimbabwe's Governance Gap

Zimbabwe's governance reform agenda is advanced in policy — fragmented in execution. MDAs and SOEs operate in risk silos, reporting manually, with no live connection between NDS2 commitments and daily institutional controls.

NDS1 lessons are unambiguous: weak inter-agency coordination, delayed disbursements, inadequate monitoring frameworks, and uneven risk maturity across institutions compound to undermine delivery and erode institutional credibility.

Fragmented Risk Silos

MDAs maintain separate risk registers with no standardized vocabulary, no cross-agency visibility, and no link to strategic objectives.

14→10 Thematic areas streamlined in NDS2 — direct response to NDS1 coordination failures

Manual Compliance Reporting

Boards and oversight bodies rely on manually compiled reports — slow, error-prone, and detached from real-time operational data.

< 68% Court clearance rates — a system-wide accountability lag indicator

Strategy-Execution Disconnect

NDS2 commitments live at policy level. No auditable system links national priorities to departmental action plans, risk owners, and performance contracts.

67.7% Average Citizen Satisfaction Index under NDS1 — below the 70% Vision 2030 target
02 — The Solution

The Governance Waterfall

GRC-Nexus uses a national-to-operational cascade so that no activity is detached from strategic purpose. Every risk, control, action, and performance measure maps to an approved objective.

This is not a reporting tool. It is a governance execution engine — converting policy commitments into daily operational controls with a complete, immutable audit trail from NDS2 priorities all the way to statutory compliance evidence.

03 — Platform Capabilities

Eight Integrated Modules

A unified stack covering every dimension of institutional governance — from strategic planning to internal audit, board management to vendor risk.

MODULE 01
Strategic Planning & NDS2 Alignment
Central NDS2 pillar library with mandatory objective-tagging. KPI baselines, targets, owners, and automated strategic review triggers on exception.
MODULE 02
Enterprise Risk Management
Risk registers linked to strategic objectives. 5×5 likelihood-impact scoring, appetite thresholds, treatment workflows, real-time heatmaps and trend analytics.
MODULE 03
Board Management & Corporate Governance
Digital board contract workflows, Section 25 evaluations, meeting lifecycle management, immutable decision audit trail, and policy repositories with annual sign-off.
MODULE 04
Compliance & Policy Management
Statutory obligations register with due-date and evidence tracking. Compliance attestations, policy lifecycle management, and real-time posture dashboards.
MODULE 05
Incident, Whistleblower & Case Management
Anonymous and named multi-channel intake. Role-based segregation, triage-to-closure workflows, and confidentiality controls for protected cases.
MODULE 06
Internal Audit & Combined Assurance
Audit universe and annual plan management, finding lifecycle with root-cause and remediation tracking, and audit committee dashboards for open and closed findings.
MODULE 07
Vendor & Third-Party Risk
Supplier onboarding with statutory compliance checks (NSSA, ZIMRA, PRAZ), third-party risk assessments, and eGP-linked contract risk obligation tracking.
MODULE 08
Reporting & Decision Intelligence
Executive dashboards with drill-down layers, statutory report generation for CGU and oversight bodies, and cross-module analytics for strategy-risk-compliance alignment.
04 — Regulatory Alignment

Built on Zimbabwe's Legal Framework

Every module is designed around the specific compliance obligations of Zimbabwe's public sector. GRC-Nexus is not an adapted generic platform — it is purpose-built for MDAs and SOEs operating under Zimbabwean law.

Compliance is enforced by design. The platform's workflow engine will not permit a critical governance step to proceed without required evidence, approvals, and documentation.

NDS2

National Development Strategy 2 (2026–2030)

Strategy cascade, KPI ownership, and IRBM performance tracking across all 10 NDS2 priority areas.

PECOGA

Public Entities Corporate Governance Act [Ch. 10:31]

Board contract workflows, Section 25 evaluations, governance compliance dashboards, and SOE oversight tooling.

PPDPA

Public Procurement & Disposal of Public Assets Act [Ch. 22:23]

Vendor statutory document verification — NSSA, ZIMRA, PRAZ registration — enforced before contract activation.

CDA 12:07

Cyber & Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07]

AES-256 encryption, immutable audit logs, RBAC with least-privilege defaults, MFA, and incident response workflows.

PSRF 2023

Public Sector Risk Framework (2023) & ISO 31000:2018

Standardized risk lifecycle, 5×5 scoring, appetite statements, treatment tracking, and ERM reporting aligned to the MoFEDIP mandate.

IRBM

Integrated Results-Based Management Framework

Performance contracts, KPI scorecards, quarterly reporting cycles, and Citizen Satisfaction Index tracking linked to OPC oversight.

24-Month Delivery Roadmap

  • Additional institution onboarding
  • Automation tuning and predictive indicators
  • Continuous control monitoring
  • Full NDS2 KPI integration at scale
