# Annex E: Program Risk Register
## ZIGDP Delivery and Operational Risks

**Version:** 2.0
**Date:** May 2026

---

## 1. Risk Rating Scale

1. Probability: Low, Medium, High.
2. Impact: Low, Medium, High, Critical.
3. Priority is determined by combined probability and impact.

## 3. Top 5 Risks for Immediate Governance Attention

1. R-01: Decision latency across institutions.
2. R-02: Data quality and trust risk.
3. R-05: Cybersecurity and resilience risk.
4. R-07: Funding continuity risk.
5. R-08: Vendor execution risk.

---

## 4. Escalation Rules

1. Any risk rated Very High is reviewed weekly by PMO and monthly by Steering Committee.
2. Any risk with Critical impact requires an active response plan within 5 business days.
3. Risks exceeding tolerance for two consecutive reporting cycles require executive intervention.

---

## 5. Monitoring Cadence

1. Weekly: program-level risk review.
2. Monthly: steering committee risk dashboard.
3. Quarterly: independent assurance and risk trend assessment.
